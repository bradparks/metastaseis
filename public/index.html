  • playback

  • cut

  • paste(only prepends to beginning of wave atm)
  • play mutiple active waves
  • reverse (reverses wave in place)
  • append (appends clipboard to end of wave)
  • prepend (prepends clipboard to beginning of wave)
  • seeking
  • drag file uploads
